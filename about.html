Our story

Started with a useful tool

Before Procellari was a company, the team built small tools for Gujarat businesses — fixes for legacy systems and internal apps nobody else wanted to touch.

Procellari Pvt Ltd registered in 2026. First product we ship publicly: a URL shortener — paste a long link, get a short one, see click counts. It's on the portfolio page.

Client work runs alongside that. One logistics dashboard rebuild cut morning load from 4s to 600ms — details on the case studies page, client name withheld.

We're not a 200-person shop. That's the point. You talk to the people writing code. No account manager in between.

Weak spots we'll own: we're new as a company. Portfolio is thin. We won't fake case studies to look bigger. If you need 50 engineers next month, we're not your firm. If you need six months of focused build work with weekly demos, call us.
