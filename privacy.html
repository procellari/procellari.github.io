Procellari Pvt Ltd ("Procellari," "we," "us," or "our") respects your privacy. This policy is designed to meet common requirements of platform providers including Google (Google Play, Google services), Meta (Facebook, Instagram, and related platforms), and Apple (App Store, iOS), as well as applicable privacy laws such as India's Digital Personal Data Protection Act (DPDPA), the EU/UK GDPR where applicable, and the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA/CPRA) where applicable.

3. Information We Collect

We may collect the following categories of information:

  • Information you provide: Name, email address, phone number, company name, job title, messages, project details, résumés (for careers), and other information submitted through contact forms, email, or client engagements.
  • Automatically collected information: IP address, browser type, device type, operating system, referring URLs, pages viewed, approximate location (derived from IP), date/time stamps, and similar technical data collected through cookies and similar technologies.
  • Mobile app data (if applicable): Device identifiers, app version, crash logs, performance data, and permissions-based data (such as notifications) only where you grant permission and as described in the app listing.
  • Client project data: Business data, credentials, and materials you provide for contracted software development, cloud, or consulting services, processed under our agreements with you.

We do not knowingly collect sensitive personal data (such as financial account numbers, government ID numbers, or health data) through our public website unless you voluntarily provide it and we have a lawful basis to process it.

4. How We Use Your Information

We use personal information to:

  • Respond to inquiries and provide requested services
  • Operate, maintain, and improve our website and applications
  • Communicate about projects, support, updates, and administrative matters
  • Process job applications and recruitment
  • Analyze usage trends and protect against fraud, abuse, and security threats
  • Comply with legal obligations and enforce our Terms & Conditions
  • Send marketing communications where permitted by law and with your consent where required

5. Legal Bases for Processing (GDPR / UK GDPR)

Where GDPR applies, we process personal data based on one or more of the following legal bases: consent, performance of a contract, legitimate interests (such as improving our Services and securing our systems), compliance with legal obligations, and protection of vital interests where applicable.

6. Cookies & Similar Technologies

Our website may use cookies, local storage, and similar technologies to enable core functionality, remember preferences, and understand how visitors use our site.

  • Essential cookies: Required for basic site operation.
  • Analytics cookies: Help us measure traffic and improve user experience (e.g., Google Analytics, if enabled).
  • Third-party cookies: May be set by embedded content or platform integrations.

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Disabling cookies may affect certain site features. Where required by law, we will obtain consent before placing non-essential cookies.

7. Third-Party Services & Sharing

We may share information with:

  • Service providers: Hosting (e.g., GitHub Pages), email, analytics, cloud infrastructure, and professional advisors under confidentiality obligations.
  • Platform providers: Google, Apple, and Meta when you interact with our apps, ads, or social integrations, subject to their respective policies.
  • Business transfers: In connection with a merger, acquisition, or asset sale, with appropriate safeguards.
  • Legal requirements: When required by law, court order, or to protect rights, safety, and security.

We do not sell your personal information for monetary consideration. We do not share personal information with third parties for their independent marketing without your consent where prohibited by law.

11. Data Retention

We retain personal information only as long as necessary for the purposes described in this policy, including to fulfill contracts, resolve disputes, comply with legal obligations, and enforce agreements. Contact form submissions are typically retained for up to 24 months unless a client relationship continues. Client project data is retained per contract terms and applicable law.

12. Data Security

We implement reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational safeguards designed to protect personal information against unauthorized access, loss, misuse, or alteration. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure; we cannot guarantee absolute security.

13. International Data Transfers

Procellari is based in India. If we transfer personal information to other countries, we will implement appropriate safeguards such as standard contractual clauses or equivalent mechanisms where required by applicable law.

15. Children's Privacy

Our Services are not directed to children under 13 (or under 16 in certain jurisdictions). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children without appropriate parental or guardian consent. If you believe a child has provided us personal information, contact us and we will take steps to delete it.
